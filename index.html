Story

Learn more about my software development journey throughout the years.

  • 2018-2019

    Introduction to Programming

    In order to complete my undergraduate thesis, I had to learn how to write code to run some regressions and analyze data. During this time, I was intrigued with the world of programming and it made me think about pursuing it as a career.

  • 2020-2021

    The Path to Self-Discovery

    Due to the lockdowns caused by COVID-19, I stayed at home for an extended period of time. This has allowed me to start studying programming on my own. I started studying Python by purchasing a course in Udemy. My study continued on until 2021 despite working in the marketing industry.

  • January 2022

    Momentous Career Shift

    I gathered all the courage that I have and decided to entirely shift my career to a different direction. I realized that in order for me to succeed in my pursuit of having an established career in programming, I had to devote all of my time into it. With this, I enrolled in an online full-stack web development bootcamp which will help me jumpstart my career in tech.

  • July 2022

    Accenture Inc.

    After months of wondering whether I made the right choice, I received a call from one of the most prominent BPO companies not only in the Philippines, but also throughout the world, Accenture. This was my first job as a software engineer, and I was awarded as the best programmer in our COBOL training. I was then assigned to work with a bank in Austria which utilizes the COBOL programming language.

  • October 2022

    Clark Staff Inc.

    In order to stay near to my family, I decided to work for Clark Staff. This position has allowed me to improve in aspects of programming such as load management, planning, and project execution since we are only a team of 3 web developers. We were tasked to make decisions on our own, which was a completely different experience compared to being instructed on what tasks to do and how to do them.
